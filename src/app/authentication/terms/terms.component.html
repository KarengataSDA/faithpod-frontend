Marketing Consent and Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Karengata SDA Church App! Your privacy and trust are paramount to us. This Marketing Consent and Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information to enhance your experience with our app. By using the Karengata SDA Church App, you agree to the terms described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:
• Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
• Usage Information: Information about how you use the app, including interaction data, preferences, and settings.
• Device Information: Information about the device you use to access the app, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
• Location Information: With your consent, we may collect and process information about your location to offer location-based services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us to:
• Provide and improve our services.
• Personalize your experience with relevant content and recommendations.
• Communicate with you about events, updates, and announcements.
• Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality and user experience.
• Send you marketing materials, newsletters, and promotional content with your explicit consent.

3. Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may use your personal information to send you marketing communications. You can opt-in to receive:
• Newsletters with inspiring messages and church news.
• Notifications about upcoming events, activities, and special services.
• Opportunities to participate in community outreach and volunteer programs.
You can manage your preferences or withdraw your consent at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

4. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our app, conducting our services, or managing our communications, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
• Access the personal information we hold about you.
• Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
• Withdraw your consent to receive marketing communications.
• Request restriction of processing your personal information in certain circumstances.
To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@karengatasda.org

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.

Acknowledgement

Thank you for trusting Karengata SDA Church with your personal information. We are committed to respecting your privacy and providing a safe and enriching app experience. By using this App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Marketing Consent and Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
